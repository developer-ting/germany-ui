Financial & Operational Restructuring

Our practice offers critical assistance to companies that are underperforming, are in a crisis, or are healthy, but with underperforming divisions. We collaborate with company management and serve in interim roles to stabilize financial and operational performance by developing and implementing comprehensive profitability and working capital plans. A&M’s involvement reassures creditors and investors that the company is taking steps to address its problems and maximize its value.
